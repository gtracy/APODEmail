Astronomy Picture of the Day (APOD)

Get APOD in your email inbox each day!

This project was originally started for my father when I was in grad school. He wanted a more efficient way of viewing NASA's APOD site every day. Since, at the time, RSS had not really taken off, I wrote a little script that scraped the APOD page and packaged it in a nice little email for him.

Now it's been nine plus years and there are {{ counter }} people on the distribution list. Who knew!?
